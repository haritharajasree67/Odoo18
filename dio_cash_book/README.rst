==============================
Cash Book – Odoo 18
============================

.. image:: banner.png
:alt: Cash Book Details
:align: center


The Account Cash Book in Odoo 18 Accounting provides a detailed record of all cash transactions made through the cash journal.
It displays the movement of cash by showing the date, journal, partner, reference, entry details, debit, credit, and running balance for each transaction.

This report helps businesses monitor cash inflows and outflows, track payments received from customers, payments made to vendors, and other manual cash entries.
The running balance column shows the updated cash position after every transaction, making it easy to understand the available cash at any point in time.

The Cash Book also includes filter options and export features (XLSX/PDF),
allowing users to analyze cash transactions for a specific period and generate
reports for accounting and auditing purposes.
