Cash Book

This report helps businesses monitor cash inflows and outflows, track payments received from customers, payments made to vendors, and other manual cash entries. The running balance column shows the updated cash position after every transaction, making it easy to understand the available cash at any point in time.

2.Under the reporting menu click on the Cash Book View

3. Here You can see the cash book details. You Have download Option and filter options also.
