Global Discount in Invoice

Boost your invoicing efficiency with easy Global Discount management. Apply percentage-based discounts to customer invoices through a simple wizard and see instant, accurate updates in invoice totals—designed for faster billing and a smoother accounting workflow.

1.Navigate to the Accounting module.

2.Under Customers, select the Invoices menu.

3.You can view the list of invoices; click New to create an invoice.

4.After entering the invoice details, click the Global Discount button to apply a discount.

5.A popup wizard will appear where you can enter the discount percentage and click Apply.

6.The discount will be applied to the invoice and reflected in the totals.
