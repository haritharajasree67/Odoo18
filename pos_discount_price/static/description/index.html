POS Discount Price

Enhance your POS experience by applying discounts as a fixed amount rather than just percentages, making on-the-spot price adjustments quick, simple, and flexible.

1.Navigate to the POS menu for quick and easy access.

2.Open the POS session

3.Now we can add a product for sale and click on the action button.

4.Here we need to click on the Disc Amount button.

5.We can enter the discount in amount here and click Ok button.

6.Here We can see the discount is added.
