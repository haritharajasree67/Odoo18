==============================
Bank Book – Odoo 18
============================

.. image:: banner.png
:alt: Bank Book
:align: center


The Account Bank Book in Odoo 18 Accounting provides a complete record of all transactions made through the company’s bank accounts.
It displays detailed information such as the date, journal, partner, reference, transaction entry, debit, credit, and the running balance for each bank transaction.

This report helps businesses track money received in the bank and payments made from the bank,
including customer payments, vendor payments, write-offs, and other accounting entries.
The running balance column shows the updated bank balance after every transaction, giving a clear view of the available funds in the bank account at any time.

With filter options and export features (XLSX and PDF),
users can easily analyze bank transactions for specific periods,
review financial activities, and generate reports for accounting, auditing, and
financial management purposes.
