Bank Book View

This report helps businesses track money received in the bank and payments made from the bank, including customer payments, vendor payments, write-offs, and other accounting entries. The running balance column shows the updated bank balance after every transaction, giving a clear view of the available funds in the bank account at any time.

2.Under the Reporting Menu Click on the Bank Book view.

3. Here You can see the bank book details. You Have download Option and filter options also.
